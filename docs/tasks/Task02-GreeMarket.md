## GreenMarket – Organic Products Shopping Application

Consumers can purchase fruits, vegetables, and other organic products directly from small producers.  
The app allows users to track the origin of products and offers a subscription system for weekly deliveries.  
In addition, consumers can interact with producers through an integrated chat, asking questions about cultivation methods, certifications, and best practices for food storage.

**Possible features:**  
- Producer geolocation  
- Order history  
- Recurring subscription  
- Product reviews
